About Us

A Place to Belong Since 1945

Founded on March 4, 1945, Riverview Baptist Church has been a cornerstone of the Clinton community for over 80 years. Whether you are searching for faith, deepening your walk with Christ, or simply looking for a family — you will find your place here.

Our Pastor

Pastor Mark Kitts

Filled with the Holy Spirit and devoted to advancing God's Kingdom, Pastor Kitts answered his calling to ministry early in life. His heart beats for the marginalized — those experiencing hopelessness, desperation, and spiritual need.

"Your current situation is not your final destination."

Drawing from the life of Job and the certainty of Christ's redemptive work, Pastor Kitts reminds the congregation that their past is covered and their future is secured in Him.

Pastor Mark Kitts
